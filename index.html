PicMap

Introduction

PicMap aims to provide a new experience of sharing photos of a trip on social media. We let user pick their photo on facebook by using facebook graph API. Allowing them to put the photo on the map using google map API. Finally, users can make the animation showing the photo on the map by the order of time.

I SEE

Introduction

Most of the college student has difficulty during the interview. We interview several college students, and after a series of iterative design which included a process of divergence, convergence, prototyping, usability testing and refining, we proposed I SEE, a mock interview system to help students ace interviews. I SEE includes customized question list, mock interview scenarios, and peer review with the online community that makes users make progress through effective practice and discussion. The project was run by User-Center Design and submit to CHI Student Design Competition.
